builtin rtw88 driver does not work well with my usb wifi tplink archer t4u
blocklist it
create /etc/modprobe.d/rtw88-blacklist.conf
add:
blacklist rtw88_8822bu
blacklist rtw88_8822b
blacklist rtw88_usb
blacklist rtw88_core
install: https://github.com/RinCat/RTL88x2BU-Linux-Driver
create: /etc/modprobe.d/88x2bu.conf
add:
options 88x2bu rtw_switch_usb_mode=2 rtw_vht_enable=1 rtw_power_mgnt=0 rtw_enusbss=0 rtw_led_ctrl=1 rtw_stbc_cap=0 rtw_ldpc_cap=0 rtw_ampdu_amsdu=0
apply to boot image: sudo mkinitcpio -P
